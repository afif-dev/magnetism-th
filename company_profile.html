  Magnetism Industries Sdn. Bhd. was established in Penang, Malaysia since 1995.  
 

Magnetism Industries Sdn. Bhd. manufacture different varieties of stainless steel wire mesh conveyor belts , which will meet any of your specific application. Our wire mesh conveyor belts offer many rational solutions for conveyance of mass and piece goods and products, especially when simultaneous treatments, that is double functions, such as washing, spraying, drying, cooling, heating, painting, etc. are required, they are also extremely practical to be used in continuous heating ovens at temparature of up to 1200 deg celsius.

We have supplied the wire mesh conveyor belts to many industries, such as food processing and bakery products, metal working, electronics etc. The success in countless applications using our wire mesh conveyor belts proved that they are flexible, reliable and durable.
 
 


Magnetism Industries Sdn. Bhd. specializing in manufacturing all kinds of metal products. We devoted to R&D of complex fasteners in 2002 and housed manufacture factory in Taiwan in 2003 to supply products to worldwide customers. Complex fasteners are basically on strength and stability of connection.

We guarantee our products for extremely high stability, especially DIN 7504 self-drilling screws are standing for our first-string products. We provide customers with stable quality, promp delivery and good service.
